Sigfides vs Local SEO Tools

Sigfides is not a replacement for BrightLocal, Yext, Local Falcon or citation tools — it fixes the identity, NAP and proximity problems those tools cannot see.

Local SEO tools work best only when your identity is already correct

Most tools assume your business identity is already stable. In reality, many businesses have:

  • inconsistent names across GBP, website & directories
  • old addresses still indexed & crawled
  • multiple phone formats or historic tracking numbers
  • coordinates & pin locations that don’t match reality
  • schema data that contradicts what’s on the page

When these signals conflict, tools collect and amplify confusing versions of your business.

What Sigfides does that tools do not

Sigfides aligns the structural signals that determine trust:

  • Canonical NAP identity (one true name, address & phone)
  • Verified coordinates & entrance-accurate pins
  • Schema that matches real-world identity
  • GBP URL & landing page coherence
  • Crawl & sitemap signal integrity

Once identity is canonical, tools become:

More accurate More reliable Less noisy

How Sigfides & popular tools work together

After Sigfides alignment:

  • BrightLocal audits reflect the correct entity
  • Yext syncs & listings reinforce one identity
  • Local Falcon grid results stabilise around truth
  • GBP edits stop fighting conflicting sources

Sigfides prepares the foundation — tools then build on a version of your business Google actually trusts.
