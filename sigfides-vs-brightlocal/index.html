Sigfides vs BrightLocal

BrightLocal is a powerful reporting & citation platform — but it assumes your identity, NAP and schema are already correct. Sigfides fixes those foundations first.

BrightLocal is valuable — but it measures what already exists

BrightLocal is excellent at:

  • citation discovery & audits
  • GBP performance & ranking snapshots
  • review monitoring
  • local search reporting

But BrightLocal does not:

  • verify which business identity is correct
  • resolve conflicting NAP or address history
  • validate map pin or coordinate accuracy
  • check whether schema contradicts reality

So when identity is unstable, BrightLocal reports inconsistent or misleading versions of your business.

Where Sigfides works differently

Sigfides confirms a single, stable truth first:

  • canonical NAP identity across web + GBP
  • verified coordinates & entrance-accurate pins
  • schema aligned with the real entity
  • GBP & landing page coherence
  • crawl & sitemap integrity

Once that is aligned:

BrightLocal audits become cleaner ranking reports stabilise duplicate identity noise disappears

How Sigfides & BrightLocal complement each other

Sigfides fixes foundations

  • identity conflicts
  • NAP drift
  • coordinate & pin mismatch
  • schema contradiction

Then BrightLocal measures reality

  • citations now reinforce one identity
  • rank tracking reflects a consistent entity
  • GBP insights become more meaningful
  • reports become decision-grade

Sigfides makes BrightLocal data trustworthy instead of amplifying fragmented signals.
